Community Outreach

We want to hear from you!

At Ink'd, we are always looking for ways to support our community. Whether it's through sponsorships, charitable donations, or volunteering our time and resources, we are committed to making a positive impact. If you are a non-profit organization or charity in need of support, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We would love to hear about your cause and see how we can help. We are also open to sponsorship opportunities, so if you have an event or project that aligns with our values, we would be happy to discuss how we can work together. We believe that by working together, we can make a real difference in our community. So let's partner up and create something amazing. Contact us today to learn more about our community outreach programs and how we can support your organization.
