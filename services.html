Screenprinting

Screen printing is a versatile and popular method of printing onto clothing. It involves pressing ink through a stencil, known as a screen, which is stretched over a frame. Each color in the design requires a separate screen, usually four or less. This process is typically used for large orders due to the setup time involved, but it can produce high-quality prints that are long-lasting and vivid. This method can be used on a variety of fabrics, from cotton and polyester to nylon and silk. It is also suitable for printing onto a wide range of clothing items, including t-shirts, sweatshirts, and hoodies, as well as bags, hats, and other accessories. With the ability to use a variety of inks, including metallic and glow-in-the-dark options, screen printing can create striking and unique designs. To ensure the best results, a skilled screen printer must carefully adjust the pressure and placement of the ink as it is pressed through the screen. This requires expertise and precision, which is why many screen printing companies have highly trained professionals who specialize in this craft. With the right combination of artistry and technology, screen printing can create stunning, high-quality designs that will last for years.

Embroidery

Embroidery is the art of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle and thread. It can be used to create intricate designs and patterns on a wide range of items, such as clothing, hats, bags, towels, and more. Embroidery service typically involves taking a customer's design or logo and transferring it onto the fabric or material of their choice. The design is then stitched onto the fabric using a specialized machine that creates precise and detailed stitching. This process can be done on a wide range of fabrics, including cotton, denim, and even leather.At our embroidery service, we use the latest technology and techniques to ensure that your design is stitched perfectly and looks exactly how you want it to. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing high-quality embroidery services that meet your specific needs and exceed your expectations.

Direct to Print

DTP (Direct-to-Print) is a printing technique that involves printing an image directly onto a garment, without the use of any transfer papers. At Ink'd we offer DTP printing services to our customers who want to achieve a soft and natural look on their garments. One of the biggest advantages of DTP printing is its flexibility. Unlike traditional screenprinting, DTP allows for printing in multiple colors without requiring separate screens for each color. This makes it a cost-effective option for small orders or designs with multiple colors. DTP printing is also a great option for customers who want to achieve a more intricate and detailed design on their garments. With the precision and accuracy of our printers, we are able to produce even the most complex designs with great detail. Contact us today to learn more about our DTP printing services and how we can help bring your design ideas to life!

Direct to Film

DTF, or Direct to Film, is a printing method that involves printing directly onto a transparent film instead of using a traditional screen. This technique allows for high-quality and precise prints, making it a popular choice for designs with fine details or small text. DTF printing also allows for a wider range of colors and shades to be used in a design compared to traditional screen printing. Additionally, it can be more cost-effective for smaller production runs since there's no need to create and prepare screens. Overall, DTF printing is a versatile and efficient technique that can produce high-quality prints for various types of designs.
